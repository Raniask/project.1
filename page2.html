About

The diffusivity equation is a partial differential equation. The fundamental components to establish the diffusivity equation include the principle of mass conservation (a continuity equation), the law of conservation of momentum (an equation of fluid motion) and an equation of state (EOS). For some particular recovery methods, additional equations may be required. For example, thermal recovery of a heavy oil reservoir is a non-isothermal process, and the principle of energy conservation needs to be considered.
